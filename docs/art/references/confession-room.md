# Confession Room — Art References

A curated set of online references for the texture-upgrade pass on *The Confession Room* (and tonal calibration of placeholder art now). All entries are **inspiration / mood board** material — none are licensed or adopted yet. Per [[grim-griper-constraints]] the MVP ships placeholder-first; final art is a separate, license-tracked upgrade pass.

For each entry: **what's there**, **why it matters for the Confession Room**, and any **caveats** (license, perspective mismatch, off-tone notes).

---

## 1. Primary touchstone — *The Happyhills Homicide*

The locked tonal + compositional anchor for the whole project ([[reference-happy-hills-touchstone]], `docs/art/scene-composition-spec.md`). User has previously supplied a slaughterhouse exterior screenshot at `/Users/khangnguyen/.claude/image-cache/8affef59-db8a-490e-8453-bde134902b06/1.png`. Additional sources:

- **[Steam — The Happyhills Homicide](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3278760/The_Happyhills_Homicide/)** — official store page; usually the richest authored screenshot set + tone tags. *Use for:* composition grammar (foreground-silhouette layer, signage placement, warm-accent discipline). *Caveat:* genre is overt gore-slasher; we keep the *grammar*, not the gore.
- **[Giant Bomb screenshots](https://www.giantbomb.com/the-happyhills-homicide/3030-92218/images/)** — community-curated screenshot gallery. *Use for:* additional scene compositions beyond the slaughterhouse — bedroom, garage, etc., to validate that the 5-layer compositional rule (foreground-silhouette → midground → background plate → signage → vignette) holds across rooms.
- **[Horror Games Wiki — Happyhills Homicide](https://horror-games.fandom.com/wiki/The_Happyhills_Homicide)** — fan wiki; mixed-quality but sometimes carries level lists / character art.
- **[RAWG — The Happyhills Homicide](https://rawg.io/games/the-happyhills-homicide)** — game database; usually has dev-tagged screenshots and short blurbs.
- **[Stash — The Happyhills Homicide](https://stash.games/games/the-happyhills-homicide)** — game tracker page; supplementary screenshots.
- **[Kotaku — Happyhills Homicide gallery](https://kotaku.com/games/the-happyhills-homicide/gallery)** — editorial gallery; useful if Steam shots are sparse.
- **[Glitchwave — Happyhills Homicide](https://glitchwave.com/game/the-happyhills-homicide/)** — review/rating page; tagged as *stealth / graphic adventure / reverse horror / side view* — confirms the genre cocktail we're cribbing structurally.
- **[Glitchwave — Happyhills Homicide 2: Out For Blood](https://glitchwave.com/game/the-happyhills-homicide-2-out-for-blood/)** — sequel; useful for seeing where the studio refined the formula.

*Tonal note for the Team Lead voice:* Happyhills carries overt gore; *Reaper's Debt* does **not**. We crib the **compositional grammar** and the **stalker-cam framing**, never the visceral content. See `docs/art/scene-composition-spec.md` §"Anti-slasher discipline".

*Deep-fetch (2026-05-30) of the Steam page:* dev framing is sparse — confirmed pixel + cartoony aesthetic, 1980s setting, "20 completely varied levels," tone tags include *"dark humor and horror movie references," "gore and guts,"* content warnings cite *"mutilation, slow murder, sadism, burning alive, decapitation, impaling"* rendered cartoonily. **No level-title list** is published on Steam — for the canonical 20 "Tape" titles (Lawn Enforcement etc., per the original persona) we would need to dig into a community wiki or YouTube longplay. Not blocking for the texture-upgrade pass; flagged for whoever picks up the post-MVP roster expansion.

## 2. Chapel interior — direct-match tilesets

For the texture-upgrade pass after MVP. None adopted; license + side-view fit needs checking on each.

- **[Candle Cathedral (16x16 Gothic Dungeon Tileset, Zaebucca, itch.io)](https://zaebucca.itch.io/adventure-below-candle-cathedral)** — *"dim-lit labyrinth of carved marble, lit by many flickering flames, columns and archways with baroque altarpieces, gilded pulpits, imposing statues and shimmering lanterns."* **Content-grammar reference only — NOT adoption-eligible for this project.** Deep-fetch (2026-05-30) confirmed: 16×16 **top-down** tileset, environment-only, $7.99+, modify-OK / redistribute-prohibited. Top-down disqualifies for our side-view game. *What it's still good for:* validating the *list* of objects that read as "gothic chapel" — animated candles, chandeliers, marble/cosmatesque floor, columns, archways, stained-glass windows, altarpieces, coffins, ossuary elements. Use that list to audit whether the placeholder Confession Room is missing any silhouettes a viewer would expect.

## 3. Tonal companions — 2D dispassionate horror

Not direct visual matches; **tonal calibration** for the Team Lead voice's *"quiet dispassionate horror"* line. Use these to validate the placeholder-art feel during slice playtests.

- **[Wikipedia — *Limbo* (Playdead)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Limbo_(video_game))** — *"dark, greyscale graphics … minimalist ambient sounds, creating an eerie, haunting environment."* The grayscale + silhouette discipline is the gold standard for the *"two-glance silhouette readability"* rule in `docs/art/style-guide.md`. *Use for:* Reaper silhouette validation (is he as readable at thumbnail as Limbo's boy?).
- **[Bloody Disgusting — 5 Best 2D Horror Games of the Past Decade](https://bloody-disgusting.com/editorials/3603155/5-best-2d-horror-games-past-decade/)** — editorial list; useful to broaden the tonal sample beyond the obvious touchstones.
- **[The Gamer — Best 2D Horror Video Games](https://www.thegamer.com/video-games-best-2d-horror-titles-list/)** — additional tonal-neighbor sample.
- **[Homeschooling Teen — LIMBO and INSIDE: A Couple of Creepy Games](http://homeschoolingteen.com/article/limbo-and-inside-a-couple-of-creepy-games/)** — comparative review; useful framing for *Inside*'s **selective-color** discipline (greyscale base + one warm accent), which is exactly Happy Hills' grammar and our locked palette.
- **[GameSpot — The Gothic Sensibilities of Darkest Dungeon](https://www.gamespot.com/articles/the-gothic-sensibilities-of-darkest-dungeon/1100-6424880/)** — *"melancholic, mysterious, lonely … like the pages of an old manuscript illuminated by candlelight."* Closest editorial articulation of the *Confession Room*'s intended emotional register.
- **[The Art Of Darkest Dungeon](https://www.pointnthink.fr/en/the-art-of-darkest-dungeon/)** — art-direction longread; references Albrecht Dürer woodcuts and Mike Mignola comics as influences. *Use for:* Reaper silhouette + ghost-replay drawing language. The Mignola "high-contrast black mass with one warm rim light" mode is exactly what the Reaper-vs-Aldric value scale in `docs/art/style-guide.md` already enforces.
- **[itch.io — `Dark Dungeon` Side-Scroller Tileset rating page](https://itch.io/e/27467747/strangeboxtales-rated-dark-dungeon-side-scroller-tileset)** — community rating; tracks back to a side-scroller dark-dungeon tileset.

## 4. Hooded Reaper — silhouette + cloak references

For the Reaper placeholder + future character-art pass. The current placeholder is `24×96` body + `32×28` hood triangle, near-black on dark background — these references either validate that grammar or suggest tweaks.

- **[itch.io — Reaper (Animated Pixel Art) by SamuelLee](https://samuellee.itch.io/reaper-animated-pixel-art)** — animated 2D reaper character with red hood and scythe, *"inspiration taken from the Hollow Knight series."* **Strongest direct reference** for a side-view animated reaper. Deep-fetch (2026-05-30) confirmed: side-scroller-tagged; animation set = `idle` (passive + hostile), `running` (passive + hostile), `wield scythe`, `holster scythe`, `attack`; "name your own price"; ~32×32 sprite size (implied, not confirmed); creator's stated license: *"I drew the art myself, feel free to use it any way you like!"* — permissive, modification approved with credit. *Notes:* (a) red hood ≠ our `REAPER_HOOD #16162a` near-black; recolor before any adoption to stay on the spectral / cold value scale. (b) The passive↔hostile animation split maps cleanly to our victim FSM's `NEUTRAL` vs reaction-state split — *for the Reaper himself we don't need a hostile state* (he's the quiet consequence, not aggressor), but the *animation-timing discipline* of having dual idle/run curves is the lesson to crib. (c) Hollow-Knight-derived silhouette grammar (tall narrow hooded figure with crisp value separation) aligns with our `24×96` body + `32×28` hood placeholder.
- **[Vecteezy — silhouette of grim reaper, side view](https://www.vecteezy.com/vector-art/46028672-silhouette-of-grim-reaper-side-view-isolated-on-white-background-graphic-illustration)** — explicit side-view silhouette. *Use for:* validating the Reaper's hooded-triangle-on-rectangle reads as "reaper" at our 32×124 placeholder size.
- **[iStock — Hooded Grim Reaper illustrations](https://www.istockphoto.com/illustrations/hooded-grim-reaper)** — broad sample; useful to scan for the *"covered figure"* silhouette grammar without falling into slasher iconography.
- **[Vecteezy — Grim Reaper silhouette (vectors)](https://www.vecteezy.com/free-vector/grim-reaper-silhouette)** — 1,393 vectors; quick visual sample for shape language.
- **[iStock — Silhouette of the Grim Reaper](https://www.istockphoto.com/illustrations/silhouette-of-the-grim-reaper)** — additional silhouette sample.
- **[Dreamstime — Grim Reaper Shadow illustrations](https://www.dreamstime.com/illustration/grim-reaper-shadow.html)** — shadow-only renders; useful for the *vignette + foreground silhouette layer* discipline in `docs/art/scene-composition-spec.md`.
- **[Dreamstime — Reaper Figures](https://www.dreamstime.com/illustration/reaper-figures.html)** — broader figure references.
- **[VectorStock — Grim Reaper silhouette vectors](https://www.vectorstock.com/royalty-free-vectors/grim-reaper-silhouette-vectors)** — commercial-license source; useful baseline.

*Anti-pattern to avoid:* the iconic scythe-wielding cartoon reaper. *Reaper's Debt*'s Reaper is the *quiet consequence* — no oversized scythe iconography, no skull-grin reveal. Hood + slit + cold value, that's the read.
